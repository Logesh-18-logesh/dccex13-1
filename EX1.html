UDUMALPET

TIRUPPUR

TAMIL NADU


Poor Man's Ooty, as it is called, the Udumalpet town is one of the famous summer resorts of Tamil Nadu, surrounded by Mountains on three sides. Its name in the ancient days was "UDUMALAI" and due to its rural atmosphere,it was also named as Pettai. In latter period it was changed as "UDUMALAIPETTAI" in Tamil.


The famous district tourist spot, Thirumurthi hills, which is about 21Kms south of here would soon get a face lift,as both the union and state governments have proposals to put this spot in the state and national tourist map, by launching various developments schemes there, in course of time. Coming to the spiritual aspect, this taluk has got a number of famous temples.Near the SAINIK School South of here, a Boat house, Swimming Pool, Crocodile farm are situated.
